Oven Baked Salmon

Description

This easy to make meal is great for all you fish lovers out there. Salmon is many peoples favorite and after this meal you will understand why. Many people out for a pan seered approch but the tenderness of the oven baked might change your life.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Lemons
  • Garlic Butter
  • Salt,Pepper,Onion Powder
  • Any other personal favorite seasonings

Directions

  1. Season you salmon to taste
  2. Place salmon into an oven safe pan
  3. Cut your lemon into slices
  4. Place lemon on top of Salmon
  5. Place garlic butter on salmon and in pan
  6. Preheat Oven to 450
  7. Put salmon in for 15-20 minutes or until done
