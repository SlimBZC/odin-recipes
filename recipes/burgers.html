Burgers

Description

An americano classic. One of the many household american meals has never been so easy to make and so good. This delictable burger will be a hit with the family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 80/20% Ground Beef
  • Hamburger Buns
  • Tomato
  • Salt,Pepper,Garlic Powder and, Onion Powder
  • Cheese white american, or personal prefrence

Directions

  1. Season your ground beef to taste
  2. Roll your ground beef into evenly made balls
  3. Cut tomato into slices
  4. Flatten the balls of ground beef into a stove top pan oon highh heat
  5. Flip until evenly cooked to taste
  6. Assemble the burger starting with and ending with your buns
