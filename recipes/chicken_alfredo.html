Chicken Alfredo

Description

A very simple yet delightful dish that only takes about 30 minutes to make.This homemade chicken alfredo will become one of your favorite dishes instantly. With just 15 minutes of prep time, dinner will be from stove to table in 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast or Tenders
  • Fettuccine noodles
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Chicken broth
  • Onion Powder,Salt and Pepper
  • Garlic paste
