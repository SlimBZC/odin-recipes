Chicken Alfredo

Description

A very simple yet delightful dish that only takes about 30 minutes to make.This homemade chicken alfredo will become one of your favorite dishes instantly. With just 15 minutes of prep time, dinner will be from stove to table in 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast or Tenders
  • Fettuccine noodles
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Chicken broth
  • Onion Powder,Salt and Pepper
  • Garlic paste
  • Grated parmesan cheese

Instructions For Sauce and Noodles

  1. Cook garlic and butter in medium sized sauce pan
  2. When Garlic has browned add chicken broth and bring to simmer
  3. Add 1 cup of milk and 1 cup of heavy whipping cream
  4. Allow to thicken and season to taste
  5. Pour water into a pot and allow it to boil
  6. Once cooked add to sauce

Instructions For Chicken

  1. Cut chicken into thin strips
  2. Season chicken to taste
  3. Put chicken into pan to cook
  4. Once Chicken is cooked add to sauce
